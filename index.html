About

Brief intro about us

EduWeb is a comprehensive e-learning platform designed to provide students with access to a wide range of study materials and online tutorials. Our website offers a convenient and intuitive interface that allows users to easily browse and access the content they need, all in one place.
With EduWeb, students can access a variety of resources including textbooks, lecture notes, and practice problems, as well as participate in interactive online tutorials and quizzes. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, making it easy for students to find the information they need and track their progress as they work through the materials.
In addition, EduWeb provides a range of tools and features to help students stay organized and on track with their studies. With customizable study plans and progress tracking, students can set goals and monitor their progress as they work through the materials.
Using HTML(HyperText Markup Language), CSS(Cascading Style Sheet), JavaScript, we can make learning more easier and in a interesting way.
